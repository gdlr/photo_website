Partnerships make the Kellogg Biological Station go 'round

Can agriculture be stunningly beautiful? Of course. Turns out, all sorts of critters see agriculture as attractive, too. Researchers at the Kellogg Biological Station think about agriculture from a number of interesting perspectives: agriculture as an ecosystem, agriculture as habitat, as biofuel. I visited the site to learn about the research there, plus hoot photos of their experimental agricultural fields.
